Privacy Policy.

Last updated · May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MedMasters Collaborative LLC ("MedMasters," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you interact with our website, our coaching services, our AI hub tool, and any related products (collectively, the "Services").

This Policy applies only to information collected by MedMasters. It does not apply to information collected by your institution, your students, or any third party.

01

Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

When you contact us, register for a Service, or otherwise interact with us, we may collect:

  • Name and professional title
  • Email address (typically your institutional email)
  • Institution or affiliation
  • Course or program context (for tailoring the Services)
  • Payment information (processed by our payment processor, not stored by us)
  • Communications you send to us
  • Course content, learning objectives, and supporting documents you bring to sessions

1.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, our hosting provider and any tools we use may automatically collect limited technical information: IP address, browser type and version, device type, date and time of access, and referring URLs.

We do not currently run third-party analytics tools. If we add analytics in the future (such as Google Analytics, Plausible, or similar), we will update this Policy and disclose what is collected and how to opt out where applicable.

1.3 Course content you bring to sessions

Course materials you share with us during the Services (learning objectives, syllabi, lecture notes, study guides, document links) are treated as confidential. We use this content solely to deliver the Services you have purchased. We do not publish, sell, or share Your Content with third parties except as needed to operate the Services, such as processing it through an AI tool we use to build the Artifact.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Communicate with you about your account, scheduled sessions, and Service updates
  • Send marketing emails (only to subscribers who have opted in)
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights

We retain personal information only as long as needed to deliver the Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. 09

California resident rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

9.1 Right to know

You can request information about the categories of personal information we have collected about you in the past 12 months, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of third parties we share with.

9.2 Right to delete

You can request that we delete the personal information we have collected, subject to certain legal exceptions (for example, where the information is needed to complete a transaction or comply with a legal obligation).

9.3 Right to correct

You can request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold about you.

9.4 Right to opt out of sale or sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. If we ever begin to do so, we will update this Policy and provide a clear opt-out mechanism.

9.5 Right to non-discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights.

9.6 How to exercise your rights

To submit a request, email srennie@medmasterscollaborative.com with "California Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will verify your identity before responding to any substantive request.
